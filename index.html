2. Data Collection, Sharing, and Core Purposes

In structural alignment with the Application's official Google Play Store Data Safety grid, the following explicit data categories are dynamically processed and may be shared with verified third-party utility networks (such as Google AdMob and Firebase Crashlytics). This handling is strictly deployed for App functionality, Analytics, Advertising or marketing, and Fraud prevention, security, and compliance:

  • Personal info (User IDs): The Application handles non-identifiable internal operational strings and rotating advertising tokens (such as the Android Advertising ID / AAID). These allow ad networks to enforce frequency capping and distinguish legitimate users from automated traffic bots.
  • Location (Approximate location): Coarse, network-based regional location data is transmitted securely to ad serving utilities to render geographically appropriate and localized ad frames.
  • App activity (App interactions): Basic technical milestones—such as puzzle completion instances, clicked layout items, and gameplay navigation choices—are tracked to gauge level balancing and accurately trigger ad rewards.
  • App info and performance (Crash logs): Stack errors, unexpected runtime faults, and memory exception data are automatically formatted into technical logs following a crash to aid the Service Provider in engineering application hotfixes.

3. Voluntary Rewarded Advertisements

To provide features without charging upfront fees, the Application utilizes Rewarded Advertisements. Users retain total control and must explicitly choose to open or view these promotional ad loops. Upon completion of the video stream, the app issues specific virtual in-game rewards (such as hints, puzzle skips, or continuation multipliers) locally on the device.
