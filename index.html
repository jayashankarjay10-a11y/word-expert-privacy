Privacy Policy

This privacy policy governs the data collection practices, security protocols, and operational frameworks for the mobile application Word Expert: Unique Challenge (Package Identifier: com.wordtower.game), which was developed and is maintained on the Google Play Store by the independent developer V Jayashankar, operating under the official registered developer name 'Jay Bro' (hereinafter referred to as the "Service Provider"). This application service is provided on an "AS IS" basis.

1. Nature of Services & Digital Products

Description of Products and Digital Services Sold: The Service Provider utilizes this digital platform solely for publishing and maintaining the interactive mobile puzzle game Word Expert: Unique Challenge. The digital delivery and monetization models consist of:

  • In-App Advertising (Ad-Supported): The Application is distributed globally as free-to-play software that displays native, interstitial, banner, and voluntary rewarded video advertisements served via the Google AdMob programmatic network.
  • No Off-Platform Invoicing: This software profile does not process direct client credit cards, traditional bank wire transfers, or independent merchant-to-customer subscription setups.
  • Payment Ecosystem Processing: Any digital settlements are securely integrated and validated exclusively via the official Google Play Billing system.

2. Data Collection, Sharing, and Core Purposes

In alignment with the application's official Google Play Store Data Safety grid, the following data categories are processed and may be shared with verified third-party utility networks (such as Google AdMob and Firebase Crashlytics) for functionality, analytics, and advertising:

  • Personal info (User IDs): Non-identifiable internal operational strings and rotating advertising tokens (such as the AAID) are used to distinguish users from automated bots and manage ad frequency.
  • Location (Approximate): Coarse, network-based regional location data is used to render geographically appropriate ad frames.
  • App activity: Basic milestones (puzzle completions, layout clicks) are tracked to gauge game balancing and trigger ad rewards.
  • App info and performance (Crash logs): Technical logs following a crash aid the Service Provider in engineering application hotfixes.

3. Voluntary Rewarded Advertisements

To provide digital features without charging upfront fees, the Application utilizes Rewarded Advertisements. Users retain total control and must explicitly choose to view these promotional ad loops to receive specific virtual in-game rewards (such as hints or continuation multipliers).

4. System Infrastructure & Security Practices

Verified Partner Integration

Third-party SDK packages integrated into the system process data according to their individual privacy frameworks:

  • Google Play Services Framework
  • Google AdMob Network
  • Firebase Crashlytics Diagnostic Platform

Data Protection Declarations

  • Data Encrypted in Transit: All parameters travel across network infrastructure over an active, secure connection using advanced encryption transmission protocols (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Data Deletion Requests: Users can request manual erasure of non-local data trace IDs by contacting the developer via email.

5. Regional Compliance (Texas SB 2420 / Age Signals)

To comply with regional laws (such as Texas SB 2420), this application uses the Google Play Age Signals API. This provides a non-identifiable verification signal to our systems to ensure we serve age-appropriate ads and content.

  • We do not collect, store, or have access to the user’s specific date of birth or identity documentation.
  • If a parent or system signal indicates that ads should be restricted, this application automatically disables all advertising components to ensure full compliance with regional safety requirements.

6. Children's Data Policy

The Application does not deliberately collect tracking parameters from children under the age of 13. System monetization handles are configured to disable personalized tracking mechanisms for minor user profiles. In restricted regions, the app utilizes the Google Play Age Signals API to automatically enforce the most restrictive data-sharing settings for child users.
